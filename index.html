Biography

Ninety-Eight-Pound Weakling:

A poor orphan from the wrong side of the tracks in the days leading up to World War II, goodhearted Steve Rogers enlisted in the army to fight the Axis Powers, but met with rejection due to his small size and underdeveloped physique. Professor Abraham Erskine, a pioneer in the development of a Super-Soldier for the United States, saw potential in Rogers and whisked him into Project Rebirth, the professor’s bold experiment in enhancing the human body to its peak powers.

Power + abilities

I can do this all day

Captain America's physical strength is at the peak of human potential, allowing him to lift and carry heavy objects, as well as engage in hand-to-hand combat with superhuman adversaries.He has agility, reflexes, and coordination that surpasses the finest Olympic athletes. This enables him to evade attacks, perform acrobatic maneuvers, and engage in combat with precision.

shield wielded by the superhero

Captain America's shield

Captain America's shield stands as a symbol of resilience and justice, crafted from Vibranium, a rare and nearly indestructible metal. Circular in design and approximately 2.5 feet in diameter, the shield embodies both defensive fortitude and offensive prowess. Its Vibranium composition enables it to absorb and dissipate kinetic energy, rendering it impervious to most forms of physical attack.
